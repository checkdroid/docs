Test Creation
====================================================

We have built tools to make test creation a breeze. Typically, automation engineers spend hours to days writing test cases and our goal is bring this down to few seconds to minutes.

Here we list our different initiatives for test creation. Please reach out to us at info@checkdroid.com for new usecases.

Barista app for Android
------------------------

The Barista app takes a novel approach to use the Android Accessibility Service for observing actions on an app under test, and provides an overlay for capturing assertions/checks.
